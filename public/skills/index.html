Forge Your Raider: Understanding Skills & Progression in ARC Raiders

Your Raider's abilities are crucial for survival and success on the surface. ARC Raiders features a robust progression system centered around three distinct skill trees, allowing you to tailor your playstyle.

Character Progression Overview:

As you scavenge and survive, you'll collect items to craft, upgrade your gear, and enhance your character's inherent skills. These improvements are vital for facing increasingly dangerous threats.

The Three Skill Trees:

Survival:

  • Focus: Enhances your Raider's durability, health regeneration, and overall resilience against damage and environmental hazards.
  • Benefit: Become a tougher combatant, capable of enduring more punishment and recovering faster.

Mobility:

  • Focus: Boosts your movement speed, stamina, agility, and efficiency in traversing the environment.
  • Benefit: Outmaneuver enemies, quickly reposition during combat, and efficiently navigate complex maps.

Conditioning:

  • Focus: Improves your Raider's physical prowess, increasing carrying capacity, weapon handling, and specific combat proficiencies.
  • Benefit: Carry more valuable loot, wield weapons with greater stability, and optimize your offensive capabilities.
