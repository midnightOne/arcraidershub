Optimize Your Kit: Crafting Effective Loadouts in ARC Raiders

Your choice of loadout before entering a raid is paramount to your success and survival. Understand the components of a powerful kit and how to adapt your gear to the challenges ahead.

Essential Loadout Components:

  • Weapons: Carefully select your primary and secondary firearms based on your preferred engagement range and target types.
  • Shields: Crucial for personal defense against both ARC machines and hostile Raiders.
  • Utilities: Equip essential items like bandages for health, shield rechargers for sustained defense, and various gadgets for tactical advantages.

Loadout Types:

Free Loadouts:

Available for risk-free runs, providing basic gear at no cost. Ideal for exploring new maps without fear of losing valuable personal items. Note: Free Loadouts do not include a Safe Box.

Personal Gear & Safe Box:

When you bring your own equipped gear, you gain access to a single Safe Box slot. Any item placed here is saved, even if you are eliminated during the raid, making it vital for securing rare blueprints or high-value loot.

Example Loadout: The "Anvil Precision" (Budget Hard-Hitter)

Concept: This simple, cost-effective loadout focuses on delivering significant damage with precision, making it an excellent choice for players confident in their aim and looking for economical power.

Key Weapon: Anvil (Hand Cannon)

  • Description: A single-action hand cannon with high damage output and headshot potential. While its handling is slow, its stopping power makes it effective against even heavily armored ARC units.
  • Effectiveness: Very effective against ARC armor plating.
  • Ammo Type: Heavy Ammo

Strategy: Pair the Anvil with a complementary utility or a faster-firing secondary for close-quarters emergencies, capitalizing on its punch for critical engagements.
