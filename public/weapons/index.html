The ARC Raiders Armory: Your Complete Weapon Database

Arm yourself with knowledge! This is your ultimate resource for every weapon available in ARC Raiders. Explore detailed statistics, understand their effectiveness against different ARC machines, and find the perfect firearm to complement your playstyle.

Weapon Categories & Highlights:

Assault Rifles:

Versatile and balanced for various combat ranges.

  • Kettle: Semi-automatic, quick and accurate, but with low bullet velocity.
  • Rattler: Fully automatic, affordable, unique 2-bullet reload.
  • Arpeggio: A 3-round burst rifle with decent damage and accuracy.
  • Tempest: Fully automatic with moderate fire rate and accuracy.

Battle Rifles:

Powerful, high-damage options, particularly effective against armored targets.

  • Ferro: Heavy break-action rifle, single-shot, very effective against ARC armor.
  • Renegade: Lever-action, high damage, accuracy, and headshot potential, somewhat effective against ARC armor.

Light Machine Guns (LMGs):

Designed for sustained suppressive fire with large magazines.

  • Torrente: High-capacity (60 rounds) semi-automatic LMG, moderate fire rate.

Pistols:

Reliable secondary weapons for emergencies or specific tactical uses.

  • Hairpin: Single-action with a built-in silencer, ideal for stealth.
  • Burletta: Semi-automatic, decent damage/accuracy, fire rate limited by trigger pull.
  • Anvil: Hand Cannon, high damage/headshot, slow handling, very effective against ARC armor.
  • Venator: Semi-automatic, fires two shots per shot.

Shotguns:

Devastating at close range, with high damage output.

  • Il Toro: Pump-action, large bullet spread, high damage at close range, sharp falloff.
  • Vulcano: Semi-automatic, good bullet spread, sharp falloff.

Sniper Rifles:

Precision weapons for long-range engagements and high-value targets.

  • Osprey: Scoped bolt-action, reliable damage and accuracy.
  • Jupiter: Bolt-action, exceptional damage/accuracy, slow handling, very effective against ARC armor.

Submachine Guns (SMGs):

High fire rate weapons for close-to-medium range combat.

  • Stitcher: Full-auto, good damage but low fire rate, can be hard to control.
  • Bobcat: Full-auto, high fire rate but low accuracy.

Special Weapons:

Unique and often experimental firearms with distinct capabilities.

  • Equalizer: High-capacity experimental beam rifle, extremely effective against ARC armor and limbs.

Understanding Weapon Stats:

  • Damage: Raw damage output.
  • Fire Rate: How quickly the weapon fires.
  • Range: Effective engagement distance.
  • Stability: How much the weapon recoils.
  • Agility: How quickly you can move or aim down sights.
  • Stealth: How much noise the weapon generates.
  • Weight (KG): Impact on overall encumbrance.
  • Mod Slots: Number of available slots for weapon attachments.
