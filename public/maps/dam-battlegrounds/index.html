Dam Battlegrounds

The foundational map of ARC Raiders, offering diverse terrain from open areas to vertical zones and close-quarters sections. Master this battlefield to dominate the extraction shooter.

Map Overview

Why Start Here

  • Always available - no rotation timers to worry about
  • Balanced difficulty perfect for learning the game
  • Multiple extraction points for flexibility
  • Consistent loot spawns for reliable farming
  • Mix of PvE and PvP encounters at medium intensity

Key Features

Terrain Diversity

The Dam Battlegrounds features a variety of environments that force you to adapt your tactics:

  • Open Areas: Long sightlines favor ranged weapons and careful positioning
  • Vertical Zones: Multi-level structures require awareness of high ground advantages
  • Close-Quarters Sections: Tight corridors and rooms where SMGs and shotguns excel
  • The Dam Structure: Central landmark that serves as a high-traffic PvP zone

Extraction Points

Multiple extraction points scattered across the map provide flexibility for different raid strategies. Learn the location and timing of each extraction to optimize your loot-and-leave efficiency.

Loot Distribution

  • Safe Zones: Lower-risk perimeter areas with basic loot and minimal enemy presence
  • Medium Risk Areas: Scattered throughout the map with decent loot and moderate combat
  • The Dam (Hot Zone): Central high-traffic area with premium loot and heavy PvP activity

Tactical Recommendations

For Beginners

  • Stick to the perimeter safe zones during your first 5-10 raids
  • Learn one extraction route thoroughly before exploring alternate paths
  • Avoid the central Dam structure until you're comfortable with combat mechanics
  • Practice the 5-minute extraction rule (plan to arrive 5 minutes before closure)

For Farming Runs

  • Spawn and immediately identify the first closing extraction point
  • Plot a looting route toward that extraction while staying in medium-risk zones
  • Avoid hot zones unless you're specifically hunting for premium loot
  • Keep runs between 5-10 minutes for maximum safety and efficiency

For PvP Engagement

  • The Dam structure is the primary PvP battleground - expect resistance
  • Control high ground positions for tactical advantages
  • Listen for gunfire to locate other Raiders engaged with ARC enemies
  • Extraction points become contested in the final 5 minutes - arrive early or be prepared to fight
