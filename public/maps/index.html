Tactical Information for Map Success

Map Navigation Tips

  • Memorize extraction point locations before deploying.
  • Utilize high ground for superior visibility and strategic positioning.
  • Always plan multiple escape routes to counter ambushes.
  • Stay alert for environmental hazards and obstacles.
  • Time your movements in sync with any potential map rotation schedules.

Loot Zone Strategy

  • Safe Zones: Begin in lower-risk areas with basic loot and minimal enemy presence to build your initial gear.
  • Hot Zones: Progress to high-traffic areas offering premium loot and heavy enemy presence once adequately equipped.
  • Extraction Points: Designated areas for safely leaving the map. These are often medium-risk, with moderate enemy presence. Be wary of other players.
