Buried City

Availability: Limited Time
Risk Level: High

Map Overview

Delve into the underground ruins of Buried City, where ancient structures hide both treasure and danger. This high-risk limited-time map demands careful navigation, environmental awareness, and a preference for close-quarters combat. The underground setting creates unique tactical challenges rarely found in other ARC Raiders maps.

The Buried City's subterranean environment changes how you approach combat:

  • Limited sightlines favor close-range weapons (SMGs, shotguns, pistols)
  • Environmental hazards can be as deadly as enemy Raiders
  • Darkness and tight corridors demand heightened awareness
  • Stealth approaches are more viable than open maps

Key Features

Environmental Hazards

The Buried City isn't just dangerous because of enemies‚Äîthe environment itself poses threats. Watch for unstable structures, toxic zones, and areas with limited visibility. Environmental awareness is as important as combat skills here.

Unique Loot Tables

  • Archaeological Finds: Rare items only found in underground ruins
  • Ancient Tech: Specialized equipment with unique properties
  • Hidden Caches: Secret loot rooms requiring exploration off the beaten path

Close-Quarters Combat Focus

Long-range weapons have limited utility in Buried City's tight corridors and chambers. Optimize your loadout for close combat, quick reactions, and mobility.

Tactical Recommendations

Recommended Loadouts

  • Primary: SMG or shotgun for close-quarters dominance
  • Secondary: Pistol with silencer for stealth kills
  • Utilities: Flashlights, grenades for clearing rooms, extra bandages
  • Don't forget your hammer - it's especially effective in tight spaces

Navigation Tips

  • Learn the layout in free loadout runs before risking premium gear
  • Mark your path to avoid getting lost in the ruins
  • Extraction points can be harder to find - know your routes
  • Sound travels differently underground - use it to your advantage

Stealth Strategies

  • Darkness provides natural cover - use it
  • Silenced weapons prevent alerting nearby Raiders and ARC enemies
  • Move slowly through high-traffic areas to avoid ambushes
  • The hammer is silent - consider it for stealth takedowns
