Spaceport

Availability: Limited Time
Risk Level: High

Map Overview

The Spaceport is a high-tech facility offering some of the best loot in ARC Raiders - but you'll have to fight for it. This limited-time map features advanced technology, intense PvP combat, and significantly more dangerous ARC enemies than Dam Battlegrounds. Only deploy here with optimized gear and a solid understanding of core mechanics.

⚠️ High Risk, High Reward

The Spaceport is not recommended for beginners. You'll face:

  • Advanced ARC enemy types with enhanced capabilities
  • Experienced Raiders with top-tier loadouts competing for loot
  • Complex facility layout requiring strong map knowledge
  • Higher stakes - losing gear here hurts more

Key Features

Advanced Loot Distribution

  • High-Tech Crates: Contain rare weapon mods, advanced materials, and premium blueprints
  • Restricted Access Zones: Require keys or puzzle solutions but offer the best rewards
  • ARC Technology: Specialized loot only available in tech facilities like Spaceport

Intense PvP Zones

The Spaceport attracts Raiders looking for premium loot, which means every corner could hide a well-equipped squad. Come prepared for combat or bring stealth gear to avoid detection.

Limited Availability

The Spaceport operates on a rotation schedule. Check the map availability timer before planning your raids. When it's active, prioritize runs here if you have the gear to compete.

Tactical Recommendations

Loadout Requirements

  • Bring your best weapons - budget loadouts won't cut it
  • Pack extra shield rechargers and bandages
  • Consider bringing grenades for contested loot rooms
  • Medium to heavy shields recommended

Survival Strategies

  • Travel with a squad if possible - solo is extremely dangerous here
  • Don't get greedy - extract when you have valuable items
  • Avoid extraction points in the final 2 minutes - they become death traps
  • Sound discipline is critical - every gunshot attracts attention
