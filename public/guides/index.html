Master ARC Raiders: Your Essential Guide Hub

Welcome, Raider! This hub provides foundational knowledge and advanced strategies to help you survive the unforgiving world of ARC Raiders. Whether you're a new recruit or a seasoned veteran, our guides are designed to enhance your gameplay and secure your loot.

Core gameplay explained: A Raider's Journey (For Extraction Shooter Beginners)

Preparation in Speranza:

  • Gear Up: Strategically choose your weapons, shields, and utilities (bandages, shield rechargers).
  • Free Loadout: Opt for a risk-free run with basic gear at no cost. Ideal for learning maps, but remember, no Safe Box here!
  • Personal Gear & Safe Box: Bring your own trusted gear to gain a crucial Safe Box slot. Items placed here are secured, even if you fall.

Exploration on the Surface:

  • Looting: Scavenge for crafting components, ammo, healing items, new weapons, and valuables.
  • Enemies (PvE): Battle the formidable ARC machines, from agile Ticks and aerial Snitch drones to the colossal Queen. Defeating them yields components and ARC power cells for shield recharges.
  • Other Raiders (PvP): Play solo, duo, or trio (max 3). Other players are your greatest unpredictable threat; exercise extreme caution. Proximity chat can lead to uneasy alliances or brutal ambushes.
  • Mobility: Utilize zip lines and the snap hook for quick traversal. Remember, running without your weapon drawn increases speed.

Crucial Extraction:

  • The Exit: To keep your hard-earned loot, you must successfully extract.
  • Alarm: Calling for extraction is a loud, public declaration, alerting both ARC machines and nearby Raiders.
  • Survive the Wait: You must hold your ground until your extraction vehicle arrives and safely departs.
  • Downed Extraction: A unique feature allows you to initiate and complete extraction even in a downed state, crawling to safety.

Event Guide: The Harvester (High-Risk, High-Reward)

Overview: The Harvester is a critical, high-stakes event guarded by the Queen, involving dangerous fire and electrical hazards. Success hinges on precise timing.

Step 1: Locate and Infiltrate:

  • Find It: Spot the "Harvester" marker on your main map.
  • Stealth Over Combat: The Queen guards the Harvester; you don't need to defeat her. Wait for her patrol to shift, then discreetly approach one of the three entrance doors to breach.

Step 2: Master the Hazard Cycle:

  • Fire Hazard: The entire room periodically fills with deadly flames.
  • Timing is Everything: Wait outside until the fire cycle completes. Your safe window begins immediately after the flames dissipate. Listen for audio cues warning of the fire's return, signaling you to retreat.

Step 3 (Phase 1): Power Cell Installation:

  • Retrieve Cells: Three Power Cells are found in breachable containers on the upper platform within the room.
  • Pre-Position: Crucially, before the safe window, place all three Power Cells near their ground nodes to minimize movement during the active phase.
  • Installation (Rapid Sequence): During the safe window, for each node: press the button to raise the housing, insert a Power Cell, then press the button again to retract.
  • Electrical Hazard: After the first cell, an electrical field drains shields. Back away to regenerate shields before proceeding to the next node.

Step 4 (Phase 2): Destroy Yellow Buttons:

  • Target Acquisition: Nine yellow buttons emerge from the walls. Destroy them with weapons or melee.
  • Disruption: The Harvester emits pulse charges that disrupt reloads. Stay focused and keep firing.
  • Team Play: Solo players may need to exit and reset the fire cycle. Teams should assign buttons for efficiency.

Step 5: Collect Loot and Extract:

  • Completion: All panel lines on the center console turn green.
  • Rewards: Press the three interaction buttons (same as Phase 1) to reveal three loot canisters, often containing high-value items like Legendary Blueprints.
  • Secure Loot: Immediately place any valuable blueprints into your Safe Pocket for guaranteed extraction.
