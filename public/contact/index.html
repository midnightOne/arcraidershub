Frequently Asked Questions

Are you affiliated with Embark Studios or Arc Raiders?
No, Arc Raiders Hub is a fan-created website and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Embark Studios AB. We're passionate fans creating resources for the community.
How often do you update your guides and information?
We strive to keep our content current with game updates and patches. Major updates are typically reflected within a few days, while smaller adjustments are made as needed.
Can I contribute content or corrections?
Absolutely! We welcome community contributions. Please email us with your suggestions, corrections, or content ideas. We review all submissions and credit contributors when appropriate.
Do you have a mobile app?
Currently, we only have this website, which is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices. We may consider a mobile app in the future based on community interest.
How can I report outdated or incorrect information?
Please email us at contact@arcraidershub.com with the specific page URL and details about what needs to be updated. We appreciate the community's help in keeping our content accurate.
Can I use your content on my website or stream?
You're welcome to reference our guides and share links to our content. For more extensive use, please review our Terms of Service or contact us directly to discuss permissions.
